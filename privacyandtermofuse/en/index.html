Somesh Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

1. Terms of Service

Definitions

Somesh (App): Dating/social app on iOS and Android.
Service: Liking, disliking, matching, messaging, and all related interactions.
User: Any individual who uses the app.
Membership: Account creation with email, password, age, gender, location, biography, photos, etc.
Subscription: Paid premium plans.
Somesh Like: A special like with increased visibility.
Content: Photos, bios, messages, and any data provided by the user.

Scope of Service

Somesh enables users to find compatible matches and communicate with them.

Eligibility & Age Requirement

You must be 18 or older to use Somesh. Fake accounts or misleading information are prohibited.

User Rules

  • The app must be used for lawful and ethical purposes.
  • Harassment, threats, fraud, impersonation are prohibited.
  • Requesting money, advertising, commercial activities are not allowed.
  • Sharing others’ messages/photos without permission is prohibited.
  • Misuse or exploiting technical vulnerabilities may result in account suspension.

Subscription & Payment

Premium plans follow App Store/Google Play terms. Auto-renew continues until canceled.

Disclaimer

Somesh is provided “as is” with no guarantees of uninterrupted service.

Account Termination

Somesh may suspend or terminate accounts that violate the rules.

Governing Law

Disputes are governed by Turkish law, jurisdiction in Istanbul.

2. Privacy Policy

Data Collected

Name, surname, email, gender, age, location, photos, social usernames, IP, device data, subscription/ payment data.

Purpose of Processing

  • Providing matching, messaging and all app functions
  • Improving app quality and analytics
  • Security & fraud prevention
  • Legal compliance

Data Sharing

Data is shared only with technical service providers. Never sold or shared for marketing.

Cookies

Cookies are used for performance improvements and user preferences.

International Transfers

Data may be processed on servers abroad. All transfers comply with GDPR/KVKK.

User Rights

  • Right to access
  • Right to information
  • Right to correction
  • Right to deletion
  • Right to learn data transfers

Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary and deleted after account closure according to law.

Social Media Integrations

Somesh may offer optional OAuth connections with Instagram, TikTok, and X (Twitter) to allow users to link their social media profiles. This integration is completely optional; unless the user connects an account, Somesh does not access any information from these platforms.

Data Collected

If the user chooses to connect a social media account, Somesh only collects one piece of data:

  • Username (Instagram / TikTok / X)

No additional personal information, media, messages, follower lists, or profile details are collected.

Purpose of Data Use

The collected username is used solely for:

  • Displaying the linked social media profile on the user's page
  • Helping the user introduce themselves
  • Increasing trust between matches

The username is never used for analytics, advertising, targeting, or marketing.

Data Sharing

  • No data is shared with third parties
  • Data is not used for commercial purposes
  • Data is not processed for cross-platform matching

Removing Account Connection

Users may remove their Instagram, TikTok, or X connection anytime in the app settings. When removed:

  • The username is permanently deleted from Somesh systems
  • It is no longer displayed on the user's profile

Scope of Permissions

Somesh does not request or access the following:

  • Password or authentication credentials
  • Messages
  • Photos, videos, or media
  • Follower or following information
  • Any ability to take actions on the user's account

OAuth access is strictly limited to retrieving the public username only.
